Product Overview

For portable, versatile optical excellence, look no further than the new EF 70-200mm f/4L IS II USM. It features an improved Optical Image Stabilization system that is designed to deliver up to 5 stops* of correction for clear and stable images, with the new IS Mode 3 added specifically for fast-moving, unpredictable subjects. A short minimum focusing distance of approx. 3.3 ft. helps expand the lens's operational range, enabling increased control of perspective and more dynamic close-ups than its predecessor lenses. Optimized lens coatings help reduce ghosting while a brilliant optical formula helps provide images of stunning sharpness, reduced glare and gorgeous color. All this and more in a lightweight, durable and weather-sealed L-Series lens body. Bring the EF 70-200mm f/4L IS II USM on your pursuit of your next great image.

Product Disclaimer

* Based on CIPA (Camera & Imaging Products Association) standards. Testing performed at a focal length of 200mm using the EOS-1D X Mark II digital SLR camera.
